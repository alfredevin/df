Executive Summary

Dambana Foundation has grown to over millions members worldwide since 2024. Here in the Philippines, we have over 700,000 members. I believe this is the influence and strength of Dambana Foundation.

Dambana Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to supporting underprivileged individuals and communities through a range of humanitarian and development programs. We focus on providing access to education, livelihood opportunities, medical assistance, and community development initiatives. Our goal is to uplift the lives of the poor and marginalized by empowering them with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to build a better future. Through compassion, collaboration, and action, the Dambana Foundation continues to be a beacon of hope and a symbol of positive change in the lives of many.

Empowering Lives, Bringing Hope to Communities

At Dambana Foundation, our mission is to uplift underprivileged individuals and communities through compassion, service, and sustainable development. We believe that real change starts at the grassroots level — by addressing immediate needs and creating long-term opportunities for growth and empowerment.

Our programs include educational support, livelihood training, medical outreach, and community-building initiatives aimed at breaking the cycle of poverty. Through partnerships and volunteer efforts, we reach marginalized sectors and offer them a chance to rebuild their lives with dignity and hope. Dambana Foundation is not just an organization — it is a movement of people working together to create a more compassionate and inclusive society.
